How the HTML game works

The game starts automatically. There is no title screen and no username. Your save is stored locally in this browser using localStorage, so refreshing the page keeps gold, inventory, tools, hats, guns, decor, eggs, pets, energy, market prices, and auto-miner progress.

Controls

Left Click moves/interacts. Click ore to walk to it and mine. Click mine/shop/house/dungeon/stairs to use them. WASD or arrow keys move one tile. Hold a movement key to keep walking. E or Space interacts/mines/shoots. I opens inventory, M opens market, O toggles Auto Miner, and Esc closes panels.

Mining and market

Ore has HP and pickaxe requirements. Stronger ores require stronger picks. Selling ore uses the rebalanced low market values. Market multipliers shift whenever ore is sold.

Town

The town contains the mine, shop, house, and dungeon entrance. The dungeon requires a bought gun before entry.

House

Buy furniture and machines from the shop or house menu. Owned decor appears inside the house. Fishing is the simple touch-the-fish version: move bait with up/down or W/S until it touches the fish.

Dungeon, eggs, and pets

Dungeon floors have harder cave grubs, destructible blocks, bullets, egg drops, and stairs to deeper floors. Snake eggs can drop from dungeon floor 5+, and horse eggs can drop from floor 10+. Eggs can be placed from inventory and hatch into pets after watering.
